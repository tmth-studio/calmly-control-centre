Claimant
consumer
Discloses financial harm
Mentions potential mis-selling or unfair charge to ITP contact. Unaware of Calmly at this point.
Completes intake form (CP1)
Receives Calmly intro email. Clicks link and completes CP1 — ≤8 screens. Submits supporting documents where available.
Intake Specialist
Waits for assessment
Receives "assessment underway" email with 2–5 day indicative timeline. No action required.
Reviews EV result & decides
Receives CP2 email with assessed value, confidence range, and fee breakdown (25% + VAT). Decides whether to proceed or decline. No deadline, no pressure.
Platform (records decision)
Receives demand confirmation
Notified that formal demand letter has been sent and 56-day clock has started. Can view claim timeline.
Reviews defendant's response
Notified of defendant response via platform. Receives and reviews any offer made.
Receives settlement funds
Settlement confirmation sent. Funds transferred to bank account within 3–5 working days (PP-C). Receives OP5-triggered outcome summary.

intermediary

Opens unique Calmly referral URL. Completes 10-field form in ≤5 min. Submits and receives on-screen confirmation. NTP fee: £25 logged.

Notified by platform that referral is under review. No further action required at this stage.

Intake Specialist
Calmly staff
Receives notification
Email notification arrives with referral summary and limitation flag. Claim enters intake queue.
Runs WP1 eligibility check
Opens referral in queue. Reviews pre-screen flags. Contacts claimant for any gaps. Applies WP1 criteria. Time budget: 69 min/claim (ARM). Passes or rejects.
EV Assessor
Assigns to EV assessor queue
Eligible case allocated to assessor. Monitors progress in claim management system.
Available for claimant questions
Monitors decision status in queue. Responds to any claimant queries about the assessment or fee structure.
Monitors active claim
Case moves to active status. Available to field claimant questions while 56-day window runs.
Reviews response & advises claimant
Analyses defendant response. Recommends accept, counter, or escalate. Manages escalation to FOS or litigation if defendant disputes.
If disputed: Escalates to FOS or litigation pathway. Case remains active.
Closes case
Records outcome in claim management system. Marks case closed. Triggers post-closure admin — OP5 report queue, QA sampling.

Calmly Platform
automated system
Creates referral record
Writes referral record + consent record + NTP fee log (£25). Dispatches intake notification email to Intake Specialist.
Stores intake data & triggers WP1
Sends claimant intro email. Stores CP1 intake data. Triggers WP1 workflow for Intake Specialist. Notifies ITP of receipt.
Stores assessment result
Holds EV result pending QS signature. Triggers CP2 disclosure workflow once Qualified Supervisor signs off.
Sends CP2 & records decision
Dispatches CP2 email with EV value, confidence range, and fee breakdown. Records claimant's proceed/decline decision. If proceeding: triggers demand workflow.
Pool Operator (provisional reserve)
Generates & sends CP3 demand letter
Generates formal CP3 demand letter. Sends to defendant legal/complaints team. Sets 56-day response deadline. Logs claim as active.
Defendant
Logs response & notifies parties
Records defendant's written response. Sends notification to claimant and Intake Specialist with response details and recommendation prompt.
Calculates fee & processes transfer
Calculates Calmly fee (25% + VAT). Instructs Pool Operator on net transfer amount. Logs case as closed. Triggers OP5 report entry for ITP quarterly summary.
